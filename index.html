A minimal yet feature-rich christian song book


    Discover and explore a vast collection of Malayalam Christian song lyrics on Keerthanam. Search for songs by lyrics, utilize features such as transliteration to English (Manglish), toggle dark mode, and adjust font size for better readability. Keerthanam is an open-source and ad-free platform dedicated to providing a seamless experience for accessing Christian song lyrics. Your feedback is valuable as keerthānām is in its early stages, feel free to raise any issues or suggestions on our GitHub repository.
